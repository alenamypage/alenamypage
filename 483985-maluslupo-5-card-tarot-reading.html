5 Card Tarot Reading











About:

You pose a question or concern and I will draw 5 cards in a cross pattern and explain there meaning. Using a Thoth Tarot Deck that I have owned and used for over 10 years. Readings will be delivered as .rtf (Rich Text Format) that can then be opened in WordPad or most other Windows text editing software. This way you can save the reading to read offline.



The cards basic meanings are Past Present and Feature of the question or concern being the middle left and right respectively.  The middle card being the present, shows the question or problem at hand, the card to its left, the past, shows how you got your current state, could point out things you missed, and the card to the right is the feature, It will guide you to answers. While the cards above and bellow the middle card add more insight into the reading over all. 



I will try to answer gigs with in 24 hours but life might cause small delays. Quick readings tho is one of my main goals.
